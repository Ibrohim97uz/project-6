HELLO!

NICE TO MEET YOU

I’m John Taylor, one of the top 10 photography and filmmaking artists living in Florida. The way to my passion was long – filmmaking, branding, painting, and many other exciting things. However, my digital photo manipulation and compositing turned into my own style made it possible for me to work in several areas including journalism, nature photography, art nude portraits, and others.

My photography collections take part in multiple festivals and contests. Thus, I’m sure you’ll love your photos! I have a great team of professionals that have chosen the art of photography as a lifestyle. These talented people help me make shots even more beautiful. So, meet my team!
